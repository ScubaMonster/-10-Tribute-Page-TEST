The series that changed television

Basic Info
  • The series originally ran from 1963 to 1989.
  • 1990's only had a movie, and the show returned in 2005.
  • Until 2023, there have been 15 actors playing the traveling Time Lord.
  • Jodie Whittaker, the first woman to play the part, premiered on the series 840th televised episode.
  • There have been serveral spin-offs, but also 97 missing episodes.
Records Info
  • The longest-running science-fiction television series in the world.
  • The "most successful" science-fiction series of all time, in terms of sales.
  • Worldwide, THE DAY OF THE DOCTOR held the Guinness Book of Records for biggest TV simulcast, broadcasting to 98 countries across 6 continents.
